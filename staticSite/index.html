Vocalize

The only Native Speaker you can fit in your pocket

Vocalize is an app that creates a Virtual Native Speaker from one of our many languages to give you live feedback on your pronunciation.

Accurate Results

The Vocalize app combines thousands of word samples from various audio sources to create a native pronounciation archetype. The user's recorded audio is processed against that archetype to produce a distance score.

Multiple Languages

Currently Vocalize provides virtual native speaker functionality for male and female American English speakers and male Spanish speakers. We plan on adding more languages in the hopes of providing every language learner a Virtual Native Speaker.

Multiple platforms

We believe language learning apps should not be limited by device format which is why we offer our app on mobile and desktop platforms. You can use our app for quick practice on the go or during a more formal practice session at home.

Completely Free

Mastering a second language can be expensive and the cost of supplementary study materials can mount up quickly. Vocalize is a free app that will help you achieve your language learning goals.

How it works!

How do we meaningfully gauge the accuracy of pronunciation and accent?

Data Scraping

Get Spoken Audio from Youtube

Transcribe Audio Content with Watson Speech to Text

Segment Audio into Individual Word Samples

Processing: Averaging

Use Fast Fourier Transform to Convert Word files to Frequency domain

Combine All Instance of Each Word Into One Averaged Word

This Averaged Word Becomes the Native Word Archtype

Processing: Comparison

Extract word features as Mel Frequency Cepstrum Coefficents from both user and native word files

Use Dynamic Time Warping algorithm to Compare the User Word with the archetype

The Returned Score is Based on the Closeness of the User's Audio and the Archtype
